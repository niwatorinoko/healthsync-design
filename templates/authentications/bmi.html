WHO BMI Classification

BMI Value Classification
Less than 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 - 34.9 Obesity Class I
35 - 39.9 Obesity Class II
40 and above Obesity Class III
Note: BMI is a general guideline and may not account for all body types. For example, muscle mass can affect BMI, potentially classifying muscular individuals as overweight. Additionally, BMI does not measure overall health, as other factors such as age, gender, and individual health conditions should also be considered. For a comprehensive health evaluation, please consult a healthcare professional.
